This is a steganography challenge with a tiny bit of crypto thrown into the mix.

One word is hidden in each of the given files, and must be concatenated with each others in the stated format to construct the flag.

one.mp3 can be listed to but at 1:06 the music will cut to a strange sound then cut back to the music. The track should be opened in a tool that can look at spectrographs where strange noise will reveal itself to be the first secret.

two.gif is a little complicated you must first notice the file size being abnormally large for a gif, and then change the file extension to .mp3. Then when listened to in a music player like VLC the second secret will be spoken at the end of the track.

three.png is a musical cipher encoded in motivic music cipher by Johann Bücking which when decoded by reading the musical notes in a certain pattern with reveal the final secret.

By concatenating the flags in order of the filenames you will construct the flag.
