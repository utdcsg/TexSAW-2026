Title: You can certainly try

Tag: Reversing

My Dastardly Dungeon Master is planning something nefarious. In our current campaign, we need to tell a wizard with a weird black orb inside him a secret sequence of numbers or he is going to blow up in a huge explosion and kill our adopted son Boblin the Goblin! (and the rest of Waterdeep). 

However, we know that he and his other playgroup are incredibly lazy and play autoD&D in a custom VTT pretty closely following the D&D 5e 2014 rules. In the last game the lazy group played they learned the secret code!

We have the randomization algorithm the VTT uses in the form of a .pyc file. In addition, we have all the dice rolls they did before the game they learned the secret code, as well as their statblocks and the adventure they played. Can you get the secret code?
